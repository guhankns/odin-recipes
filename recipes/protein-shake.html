Protein Shake

Description

As someone enthusiastic about resistance training, I love whey protein. It helps me cover my daily protein needs without hassle. Making a delictious shake with it also ensures I don't dread it but instead, look forward to consuming it.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Coconut milk (Other kinds of milk can also be added, but I like coconut milk the most
  • 2 bananas
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter powder
  • 1 scoop whey protein
  • Honey/Jaggery (optional)
  • 2 ml Vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the bananas into smaller pieces
  2. Put everything in the blender and blend them
  3. Add water if necessary for easier blending
